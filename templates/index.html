Why This Matters

Most PDFs and Office files contain hidden formatting, XML metadata, styling instructions and structural noise that AI models have to wade through before reaching your actual content.

AI models often perform better when given clean Markdown because the content becomes easier to parse and understand — with no wasted context window on invisible formatting.

Whether you're building a RAG system, pasting context into ChatGPT, or feeding documents to an AI agent, clean Markdown gives you a measurable edge.
